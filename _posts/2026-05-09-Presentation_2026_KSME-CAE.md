From May 6 to 9, 2026, I delivered a poster presentation at the **KSME CAE & Applied Mechanics Division 2026 Conference**, held in Yeosu, Republic of Korea.

The poster, titled **"Text-Guided 3D Multiscale Topology Optimization: TPMS-Based Anisotropic Design for Shape and Stiffness Programming,"** introduced a text-guided multiscale design framework that connects natural language with multiscale topology optimization, integrating geometric shape control with stiffness programming through TPMS-based anisotropic design.
